Meet the Team

We are a passionate team of musicians, developers, and business experts- with our headquarters based in Toronto.

Ivan Ng

Chief executive, former founder of Flock and now Business Development Strategist and co-founder of Wave. Lives for growth and cigarettes. A resolute believer that pancakes are the cornerstone of civilization and the breakfast of champions. Given the chance will drag you to the best and worst summer Hollywood blockbusters, and lives his life in black.

Joel Lee

Chief creative officer, musician, design extraordinaire and co-founder of Wave. The counterpart to Ivan, managing and executing all things creative while not destroying parties with industrial techno and italo disco tracks. Joel also lives his life in black and well known for his love/hate relationship with carbs.

Zachary Yale

Chief technology officer, programming prodigy, drinker of beers and coder on dual monitor with multi-window setup and lover of the words “wire frames and buzz words” laying waste to our ideas on the digital frontier.



Nicole Boudreau

Chief operating officer, trusted advisor, confidant and strategist, an expert in operations and people management. Beautiful as she is smart and 1/2 of the Boudreau twins. Specializing in scrutinizing Ivan’s thinking and catching his errors and calling him out.

Aidan Boudreau

Chief marketing officer, artist, designer and the future Mark Rothko helming conceptual design and social net content strategy. The 2/2 of the Boudreau twins, equally as beautiful as her talent in watercolour painting, photography and sketches, bringing nuance and depth in our execution.

Aditya Surabi

Lead backend developer, digital zen master of solitude and back-end systems programming and co-founder of Wave. Connoisseur of culturally relevant tv shows, Wu-tang die-hard and resolute stance against ever downloading Pokemon Go.
